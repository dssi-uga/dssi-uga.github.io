## Overview

Additive and Sustainable Manufacturing explores new production workflows that combine advanced additive techniques with sustainability-driven process design and quality assurance.

## Approach

- Develop monitoring systems for additive manufacturing processes to ensure consistent build quality.
- Model material behavior under different process conditions to support robust part production.
- Design sustainable workflows that reduce waste, energy use, and environmental impact.

## Goals

- Improve additive manufacturing reliability through real-time process sensing.
- Capture material and process knowledge to inform better part design.
- Enable greener production pathways with sustainable materials and recycling-aware workflows.

## Impact

This project supports the transition to manufacturing systems that are both innovative and environmentally responsible, combining advanced fabrication with quality-centered, sustainable operations.
