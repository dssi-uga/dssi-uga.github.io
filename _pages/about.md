## About the DSSI Lab

The **Data Science and System Informatics (DSSI) Lab** at the University of Georgia focuses on data analytics and artificial intelligence (AI) in advanced manufacturing, occupational safety, and healthcare systems. Our work addresses hands-on, simulation, and computational research, including human-robot collaboration, digital twin development, and AI for process modeling and control.

## Why Our Work Matters
As industries become more data-driven and automated, ensuring that advanced technologies are safe, reliable, and efficient is more important than ever. Our work applies data science and artificial intelligence to improve decision-making, enhance system performance, and reduce risks across manufacturing, healthcare, and human-centered environments. From enabling safer human-robot collaboration to developing digital twins and intelligent process controls, we aim to bridge the gap between cutting-edge research and real-world impact. By doing so, we help build systems that are not only innovative, but also trustworthy, scalable, and beneficial to society.

## Join Our Team

We are actively looking for self-motivated undergraduate and graduate students to join our lab. If you are interested in AI-enabled smart manufacturing, process analytics, or human-robot collaboration, please reach out!
