Welcome to the **Data Science and System Informatics (DSSI) Lab**!

We are located in the College of Engineering at the University of Georgia (UGA). Our research focuses on data analytics and artificial intelligence (AI) in advanced manufacturing, occupational safety, and healthcare systems. Our work addresses hands-on, simulation, and computational research, including human-robot collaboration, digital twin development, and AI for process modeling and control.

## Our Mission

The DSSI Lab bridges the gap between theoretical data science and practical engineering applications. We develop innovative solutions that enhance manufacturing processes, improve workplace safety, and advance healthcare through intelligent systems and data-driven decision making.

## Research Areas

- **AI-Enabled Smart Manufacturing**: Machine learning and IoT technologies for predictive modeling and adaptive process control
- **Digital Twins and Cyber-Physical Systems**: High-fidelity models for monitoring, simulation, and optimization
- **Human-Robot Collaboration**: Safe and efficient interaction between humans and robotic systems
- **Occupational Safety Analytics**: Data-driven approaches to workplace safety and risk assessment
- **Healthcare Systems Engineering**: Applying data science to improve healthcare delivery and outcomes

## Facilities & Resources

Our lab is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities in the 1040B I-STEM Complex, including advanced manufacturing equipment, computing resources, and testing facilities. We collaborate with industry partners and government agencies to translate research into real-world impact.
